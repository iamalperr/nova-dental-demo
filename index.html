About Us

Your Health Is Our Priority

Nova Dental Clinic was founded with a single mission: to deliver premium dental care in a warm, stress-free environment where every patient feels truly valued.

Since 2009, we have been combining clinical excellence with cutting-edge dental technology to transform smiles. Our team of specialists continuously advances their skills through international training programs, ensuring you receive the most effective and up-to-date treatments available.

  • State-of-the-art digital X-ray & 3D scanning
  • Multilingual patient support team
  • Sedation options for anxious patients
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
